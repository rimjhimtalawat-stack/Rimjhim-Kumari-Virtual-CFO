Virtual CFO Support for Manufacturing & Trading Businesses

Manufacturing and trading businesses are often profitable on paper, yet face persistent cash pressure due to inventory build-up, extended receivable cycles, and fixed operating commitments. Virtual CFO support focuses on restoring cash discipline and financial control across the working capital cycle.

Common Financial Challenges

  • High inventory levels blocking operating cash
  • Receivables extending beyond agreed credit terms
  • Vendor payment pressure and liquidity strain
  • Mismatch between reported profit and actual cash availability

How a Virtual CFO Helps

  • Working capital cycle analysis and optimisation
  • Cash flow forecasting and liquidity visibility
  • Receivables and payables discipline
  • Decision support balancing growth and cash preservation

Engagement & Pricing (Indicative)

Manufacturing and trading businesses typically require cash-intensive oversight due to inventory, credit exposure, and operational complexity. Engagements are structured as monthly retainers, aligned to business scale and control requirements.

  • Foundational Oversight: ₹10,000 – ₹30,000 / month
  • Growth & Control: ₹30,000 – ₹50,000 / month
  • Strategic CFO Partnership: ₹50,000+ / month

Final scope and pricing are discussed after an initial review based on complexity, reporting maturity, and engagement depth.
