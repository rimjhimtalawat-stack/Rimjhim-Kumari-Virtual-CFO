Virtual CFO Support for Professional Services Businesses

Professional services firms often appear profitable on paper, yet struggle with cash predictability due to billing cycles, delayed collections, and growing overheads. Virtual CFO support focuses on financial clarity and control, not bookkeeping.

Common Financial Challenges

  • Mismatch between billed revenue and cash receipts
  • Delayed client collections impacting liquidity
  • Rising fixed costs as teams scale
  • Lack of clarity between profit and actual cash
  • Founder decisions made without financial context

How a Virtual CFO Helps

  • Cash flow forecasting aligned with billing cycles
  • Receivables discipline and collection prioritisation
  • Cost structure review and fixed cost control
  • Profit vs cash reconciliation
  • Decision support for hiring, pricing, and growth

Typical Outcomes

  • Improved cash predictability
  • Better control over overheads
  • Clear understanding of financial capacity for growth
  • Reduced financial stress for founders
